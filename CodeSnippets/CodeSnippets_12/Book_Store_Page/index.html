Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

Every common man who by his sheer grit and handwork achieves success should share his story with the rest for they may find insipration and strength to go on, in his story.The 'Wings of Fire' is one such autobiography by visionary scientist Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam, who from very humble beginnings rose to be the President of india.The books is full of insights, personal moments and life experiences of Dr.Kalam.It gives us an understanding on his journey of success.

The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bagath

The 3 mistakes of my life is the third novel written by eminent indian Authour Chetan Bhagat. Based on cricket, business and religion, the novel is set against the backdrop of beatiful city Ahmedabad. Revolving around three young indian boys Omi, Ishaan and Govind, the books goes on to narrate how the three are trying their best to make ends meet in the city. Based on real events, the book starts with a dramatic twist, where Bhagat is reading an e-mail sent by some young person Govind.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter's life is a miserable. His parents are dead and he's stuck with his heartless relatives, who force him to live in tiny closet under the stairs.
